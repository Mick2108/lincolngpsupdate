How to Update Your Lincoln GPS

Follow these simple steps to update your Lincoln navigation system

1

Check Your Current System Version

Navigate to the "Settings" menu on your Lincoln's SYNC system, then select "System Information" to view your current software and map versions. Make a note of these details as you'll need them to determine which update is required.

2

Purchase the Correct Update

Visit our official website to purchase the appropriate update for your Lincoln model and SYNC version. Ensure you select the correct year, model, and system version to guarantee compatibility.

3

Download the Update Files

After purchase, you'll receive download instructions. You can download the update directly to a USB drive (minimum 32GB) or order a pre-loaded USB drive if preferred. The download process may take several hours depending on your internet connection.

4

Install the Update in Your Vehicle

Start your Lincoln and ensure it's in park. Insert the USB drive into the vehicle's USB port. The system should automatically detect the update and prompt you to begin installation. Follow the on-screen instructions carefully.

5

Verify the Installation

After the installation completes (which may take up to 60 minutes), verify the update was successful by checking the system information again. The version numbers should now reflect the latest update. Restart your system to ensure all changes take effect.
